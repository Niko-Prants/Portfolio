About Me

We are Jimi Kylliäinen and Niko Prants, graduating game engineers and long-time work partners. Jimi specializes in game technology and Niko in game programming. We have worked together for several years developing visually stunning and artistic game projects. We are particularly interested in how games can serve as a means of expression and storytelling. We have experience in producing games for both computer and mobile platforms that have reached a wide audience. We combine technical expertise and artistic work with the aim of developing high-quality game projects with meaningful content
